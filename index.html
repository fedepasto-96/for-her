Vitamin D3 → test & likely raise test

Add: Ferritin / iron test new
Lean + monthly cycle often means low iron (affects hair & energy). Test, don't guess.
Add: Methylfolate 400mcg new
Supports hormonal balance now, and lays the groundwork for fertility.
Add: Women's multivitamin new
Gently covers any gaps from intermittent fasting + a lean intake.
Vitamin C + collagen — do you need both? Not two big doses, no. But they're partners, not duplicates: vitamin C is the cofactor your body requires to turn collagen into actual skin and joint tissue. So the sweet spot is collagen + a small amount of vitamin C (a combined powder, or ~250-500mg C on the side). If you eat plenty of fruit & veg you may already get enough — the separate high-dose tablet isn't essential once you're on collagen.

🌼 What each one does

A quick one-line job description for everything in your world.

Omega-3 (EPA/DHA)
Heart, brain & skin; calms inflammation and supports hormones.
Vitamin C
Antioxidant + immune support, and the cofactor that lets your body build collagen.
Collagen
Skin elasticity & hydration, plus hair, nails and joint support.
Biotin
A B-vitamin for hair strength and nail growth.
Vitamin D3 (+ K2)
Bones, immune system & mood; K2 steers calcium into bone, not arteries.
Magnesium glycinate
Sleep, muscle recovery, calm and easing PMS.
Creatine
Strength & muscle gain — plus a little extra mental focus.
Whey protein
Complete protein to build and repair muscle.
Clear protein
Lighter whey isolate — same muscle support, easier on the stomach.
D-Mannose + Cranberry
Urinary-tract support — helps keep bacteria from sticking. Use around UTI risk.
Methylfolate
Active folate for hormonal balance and fertility groundwork.
Women's multivitamin
A safety net that fills small gaps from a lean or fasted diet.
Iron
Carries oxygen in the blood — supports energy and hair (only if a test shows you're low).
The two biggest levers aren't pills 💛 — hit your protein target, and nudge sleep from 6-7h toward 7.5-8h. Those move muscle gain and hormones more than any capsule. When you're ~3 months from trying to conceive, swap the methylfolate for a full prenatal.

General wellness information, made with love — not medical advice. Please confirm vitamin D, iron, and any new supplements with your doctor.
