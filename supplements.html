💊 Your current stack

Omega 3-6-9 swap
You don't need 6 or 9. Move to an EPA/DHA-only fish oil (~1000mg+ combined) — better for skin, hormones & fertility.
Ester-C 500mg keep
Lovely for skin and collagen.
Biotin 5000mcg keep*
Fine, but high. Pause it a few days before any blood test — it can skew thyroid & cardiac labs.
Vitamin D3 7000/week test
That's ~1000 IU/day — likely low. Get your levels checked; most do better at 2000-4000/day, paired with K2.
D-Mannose + Cranberry as-needed
This is really a UTI tool — skip the daily dose unless you get recurrent UTIs.
Magnesium glycinate 200mg keep
Great for sleep, recovery & PMS. Can go up to 300mg.
Creatine 5g keep
Top pick for muscle gain (and skin & focus). Consistency matters more than timing.

✨ Worth adding

  • Protein powder — the #1 thing for muscle gain. Aim ~75-95g protein/day total.
  • Ferritin / iron test — lean + monthly cycle often means low iron (affects hair & energy). Test, don't guess.
  • Methylfolate 400mcg — supports hormonal balance now, and lays the groundwork for fertility.
  • Women's multivitamin — gently covers any gaps from intermittent fasting + a lean intake.
